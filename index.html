Installation Guide

Follow these steps to install YouFlex on your iOS device

1

Download the IPA file

Click the download button above to get the YouFlex IPA file. Save it to your device or computer.

2

Install using sideloading tool

Use one of these methods to install the IPA:

  • AltStore (Recommended) - Install via computer
  • Sideloadly - Easy Windows/Mac tool
  • TrollStore - Permanent signing (if available)
3

Trust the developer

After installation, go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management and trust the developer certificate.

4

Open YouFlex

Launch the app and enjoy your enhanced YouTube experience with all 20+ tweaks!

Important Notice

YouFlex is a modified application and may require re-signing every 7 days with a free Apple Developer account, or 1 year with a paid developer account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about YouFlex installation and usage

Is YouFlex safe to use?
Yes, YouFlex is safe and only modifies the YouTube app to enable additional features. All tweaks are based on open-source projects from trusted developers. We don't collect any personal data.
Why do I need to reinstall every 7 days?
This is an Apple limitation for sideloaded apps with free developer accounts. Paid developer accounts can sign for 1 year. TrollStore users can install permanently if available for their iOS version.
Can I use my YouTube account?
Yes, you can log in with your Google account normally. All your subscriptions and history will sync. The tweaks work alongside your regular account.
Will I get banned from YouTube?
There is very low risk of account bans as these modifications are client-side only. However, we cannot guarantee 100% safety as it violates YouTube's Terms of Service.
Does it work on iPad?
Yes, YouFlex works on both iPhone and iPad devices running iOS 14.0 or later. All features are optimized for both device types.
How often is YouFlex updated?
We regularly update YouFlex to include the latest tweaks and maintain compatibility with new YouTube versions. Follow our GitHub for update notifications.
